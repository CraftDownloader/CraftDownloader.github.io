Capture the Flag

Minecraft Minigame

Description

Capture the Flag is a team-based Minecraft minigame where players must infiltrate the opposing base, steal their flag, and return it to their own base. Matches are time-limited, and teamwork is key to victory.

Gameplay Guide

Tips & Strategies

  • Coordinate with teammates to defend your own flag.
  • Use stealth and strategies to avoid opponents.
  • Know the map layout to plan the fastest routes.
