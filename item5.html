King of the Hill

Minecraft Minigame

Description

King of the Hill is a multiplayer Minecraft minigame where players compete to stay on a designated hill the longest while others attempt to knock them off. The last player standing wins.

Gameplay Guide

Tips & Strategies

  • Focus on positioning and timing to control the hill.
  • Watch other players closely to anticipate attacks.
  • Use defensive maneuvers to block opponents from climbing.
  • Stay aware of power-ups or items on the hill.
