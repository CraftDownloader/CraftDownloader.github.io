Prop Hunt

👥 2–10 Players 🎮 Bedrock Edition 🪑 Hide as a Block

Description

Prop Hunt is a fun skill-based hide and seek game where the goal is to hide as a block and survive the hunter.

Tips & Strategies

  • Focus on positioning to avoid the hunter's attention.
  • Even if the hunter is close, don't give your spot away.
  • If you're the hunter, look for blocks that don't seem right.
