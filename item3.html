Spleef

Minecraft Minigame

Description

Spleef is a fast-paced Minecraft minigame where players compete to break blocks beneath opponents and make them fall.

Gameplay Guide

Tips & Strategies

  • Focus on isolating a single opponent at a time.
  • Keep moving to avoid being an easy target.
  • Learn arena layouts to anticipate where blocks will break.
